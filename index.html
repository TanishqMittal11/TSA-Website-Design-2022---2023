This is an animated TV show, rated TV-Y, that tells the story of five childhood friends who embark on a thrilling space adventure that takes a turn for the worse.
Introducing a new and thrilling T.V. Show where a group of children stumbles upon an unearthly object in their local park. Follow their journey as they innocently approach the UFO only to find themselves trapped inside. With limited resources and no adult supervision, the children must work together to uncover the mysteries of the ship and find a way back home. Will they be able to overcome their fears and escape the clutches of the unknown entity? Tune in to find out!
